Adding Your Tax Registration Number to Invoices

Learn how to enter your GST/HST or other tax registration number once in Company Settings, and how the display toggle controls whether it automatically appears on your invoices.

Applies to all accounts

1

What This Feature Does

Many painting contractors, particularly in Canada, are required to display a tax registration number (such as a GST/HST number) on invoices. Previously, DripJobs had no dedicated place to store this, so it had to be typed into invoice notes manually on every single invoice.

Now, Company Settings includes a Tax Information section where you enter your registration number once. A toggle controls whether that number automatically renders on your invoices going forward, so you never have to add it manually again.

💡
Where to Find It

This lives inside Company Settings › Company Information, as a new card in that tab. It is not a separate settings tab, so no new navigation item to hunt for.

2

Setting Up Your Tax Registration Number

  1. From the left sidebar, go to Company Settings
  2. Select the Company Information tab
  3. Scroll to the new Tax Information card, positioned after Company Details and before Addresses
  4. Enter your registration number in the text field (free-text, no specific format required)
  5. Turn on the Show tax registration number on invoices toggle if you want it to appear automatically
  6. Save your changes
The Tax Information card in Company Settings › Company Information, shown here for a Canadian account with the toggle on and a number entered.
✅
No Format Requirements

The field accepts free-text entry, so it works for a GST/HST number, a VAT number, a state tax permit, or any other registration format your business uses.

3

How the Invoice Display Toggle Works

The Show tax registration number on invoices toggle is what actually controls whether your number renders anywhere. Entering a number by itself does nothing until the toggle is turned on.

Toggle StateField Has a ValueResult
OffYes or NoNumber never appears on any invoice surface
OnYesNumber appears on all invoice surfaces
OnNo (empty)Renders as if the toggle were off, no blank space is left behind

The toggle defaults to off for every account, so nothing changes on your invoices until you turn it on intentionally.

Inline Guidance While You Set It Up

Company Settings shows one of two inline messages depending on your current setup, so you always know what customers will see:

"The label 'GST/HST #:' (or 'Tax Reg #:') and your registration number will appear in the company information block at the top of every invoice PDF."

Shown when the toggle is on and the field has a value.

"No registration number entered. The toggle is on, but no number has been entered. Invoices will render as if the toggle is off."

Shown when the toggle is on but the field is empty.

When the toggle is on but the field is empty, the number is treated as if the toggle were off, and this warning appears in Company Settings.
⚠️
Only One Message Shows at a Time

The confirmation hint and the warning are mutually exclusive, and both are hidden entirely when the toggle is off.

Where the Number Appears on Invoices

Once the toggle is on and a number is entered, it shows up consistently across every place an invoice can be viewed or downloaded:

Invoice PDF and Customer Portal PDF

Renders in the company information block at the top of the document, directly below your company name, address, phone, and email.

Excerpt from an Invoice PDF. The registration number sits in the company information block, just below the contact details, using the country-appropriate label.
Invoice View Inside DripJobs

A Tax Registration row appears in the invoice meta card so your team can confirm at a glance what's being shown to the customer.

Customer Portal Invoice View

The number renders in the company information block that customers see when reviewing or paying an invoice online.

The customer-facing Customer Portal invoice view. The registration number appears in the same company information block, visible to the customer before they pay.

This applies to every invoice type, standard invoices, deposit invoices, and anything viewed or downloaded through the Customer Portal.

Common Questions & Troubleshooting

I turned the toggle on, but I don't see the number on my invoice.

Check that a value is actually saved in the Tax Registration Number field. If the field is empty, the toggle has no effect, and an inline warning will be visible in Company Settings to let you know.

Can I show the number on some invoices but not others?

Not currently. The toggle is an account-wide setting, once turned on with a value entered, the number appears on all invoice types (standard, deposit, and Customer Portal views).

Does this affect who can edit the field?

The field and toggle are visible to everyone with access to Company Settings, but only users with Company Settings save permissions can actually change them.

Is there a required format for the number?

No. The field accepts free-text entry in V1, so it can hold a GST/HST number, VAT number, state tax permit, or any other format.

Does turning this on affect any of my automated emails or drip sequences?

No. This only changes what's rendered on invoice documents and views. It has no effect on Drip sequences, SMS, email sends, blasts, or any scheduled outbound communication.
