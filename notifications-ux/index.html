Notifications
New Lead New
Triggered when a potential customer submits your booking form, is captured via the Contacts system, or is added by a team member as a new lead.
A new lead or contact has entered your pipeline.
New Appointment
Triggered when a customer books through your booking form or when a team member schedules a new appointment in the calendar.
A new appointment has been booked or scheduled.
Proposal Accepted
Triggered when a customer digitally signs and accepts a proposal you've sent. This is a high-value event — ensure this stays enabled.
A customer accepted a proposal you sent them.
Proposal Rejected
Triggered when a customer explicitly declines a proposal by clicking the "Reject" option on the customer portal.
A customer rejected a proposal you sent them.
Proposal Viewed
Triggered when a customer opens a proposal link. By default, this fires on first view only. You can adjust frequency below.
A customer opened a proposal link.
Invoice Viewed
Triggered when a customer opens an invoice link. By default, this fires on first view only to reduce notification noise.
A customer opened an invoice link.
Invoice Paid
Triggered when a customer completes a payment on an invoice, either online via Stripe or marked as paid manually by a team member.
A customer paid an invoice — online or manually marked as paid.
Chat Message Received
Triggered when a customer or team member sends a new message in the DripJobs chat or messaging system.
A new message was received in the DripJobs chat.
Unsaved changes
