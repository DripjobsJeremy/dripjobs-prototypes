# DripJobs Weekly Product Update

**Period:** July 6 – July 10, 2026
**Release:** Jul 8 deployment · 10 updates

---

## Sales Pipeline

### Deal Cards Show a Proposal Link Even Before It's Sent — *Jul 8*
**What changed:** Fixed a bug where a deal card in the "Needs Review" stage would show the appointment date instead of a "View Proposal" link when a proposal existed but hadn't been sent yet.
**Why it matters:** You need a reliable one-click path to a proposal directly from the pipeline, whether it's been sent or not.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Metrics & Reports

### Metric Breakdown Slide-Out Counts Are Now Clickable — *New · Jul 8*
**What changed:** Numbers shown in the Metric Breakdown slide-out on your Metrics Dashboard now link directly to the full detail report for that category, pre-filtered and pre-dated to match what you were viewing.
**Why it matters:** Skip the manual filtering — one click takes you straight to the records behind any number.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Messaging & Automations

### New First Name Keywords for Drips, Reminders & Templates — *New · Jul 8*
**What changed:** Added two new dynamic keywords — `{salesperson-first-name}` and `{user-first-name}` — available anywhere you already use keywords: drip sequence steps, reminders, and message templates.
**Why it matters:** Write messages that sound like a real person talking, using first names instead of full names.
**Action needed:** Update your templates if you'd like to start using the new keywords.

### DJ Chat Search Now Highlights Your Matched Terms — *Jul 6*
**What changed:** When you search across DJ Chat conversations, the word or phrase you searched for is now highlighted directly in the results.
**Why it matters:** Find what you're looking for at a glance instead of scanning every result line by line.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Proposal Builder

### Fixed Garbled Character in Change Request Status Badge — *Jul 8*
**What changed:** The proposal status badge no longer shows a stray character where the separator dot should be when a proposal is in a "Change Request" state.
**Why it matters:** A clean, correctly rendered status badge everywhere it appears — no visual noise.
**Action needed:** None

### Customer Views and Work Orders Now Show the Right Labels — *Jul 8*
**What changed:** Customer-facing views and PDFs now show only the client label, while work orders show both labels since they're often shared with crews and contractors.
**Why it matters:** Customers only see the label that makes sense for them, and your crew still gets full context on work orders.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Jobs & Sales Lists

### Customer Name Column Now Freezes While Scrolling — *New · Jul 8*
**What changed:** The Customer Name column (and everything to its left) now stays fixed in place as you scroll horizontally in the Jobs List and Sales List. The Jobs List "Name" column was also renamed to "Customer Name" for consistency.
**Why it matters:** You never lose track of which customer's row you're looking at, even in a wide table.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Mobile App

### Android: Client Notes Save Button No Longer Hidden — *Jul 8*
**What changed:** Fixed an issue on Android where the Save button for Client Notes in a proposal was partially hidden and hard to tap.
**Why it matters:** Saving a client note from the field shouldn't be a fight with your keyboard.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Financing

### Acorn Financing Now Available for Massachusetts Tenants — *New · Jul 8*
**What changed:** Acorn Finance is now live for Massachusetts-based tenants, with the required $6,700 minimum loan amount enforced in the financing flow.
**Why it matters:** MA tenants can now offer financing on proposals the same way tenants in other supported states already can.
**Action needed:** None

---

## Admin Settings

### Schedule Training URLs Get a New Type, Icons, and Reordering — *New · Jul 8*
**What changed:** The Training URLs tool in Admin Settings now supports an "Announcement" link type with its own icon, plus the ability to reorder links so you control what shows first in the DripJobs dropdown menu.
**Why it matters:** Gives admins more control over what shows in the dropdown menu and in what order — not just scheduling links anymore.
**Action needed:** None
