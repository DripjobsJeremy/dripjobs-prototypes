## 📄 Proposals & Proposal Builder

### Proposal Section Reordering — Expanded to All Eligible Sections 🎯 — Jun 30
**What changed:** Users can now reposition more sections in the Proposal Builder than before. Position control now covers every eligible section, not just a subset.
**Why it matters:** More control over section order means proposals can be structured the way each contractor actually presents their pricing.
**Action needed:** None

### Enhanced Proposal Rejection Reasons 🎯 — Jun 30
**What changed:** The rejection reason dropdown is expanded and now includes an "Other" option with a freeform feedback field.
**Why it matters:** Clearer insight into why a customer said no helps contractors adjust their pitch and pricing going forward.
**Action needed:** None

### Point-in-Time Signed Proposal PDF — Jun 30
**What changed:** A snapshot of the exact proposal PDF is now saved at the moment a customer signs.
**Why it matters:** The signed record can never drift from later edits to the live proposal, protecting both the contractor and the customer.
**Action needed:** None
